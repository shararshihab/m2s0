About the researcher

From rigorous modeling to field-ready systems.

My work combines analytical modeling, multiphysics simulation, embedded hardware, AI, and experimental validation to solve multidisciplinary engineering problems.

At the Dr. Anwarul Abedin Institute of Innovation (D2A2I), I support research and development across IoT platforms, computer vision, robotics, technical operations, procurement, documentation, and publication. My current academic direction emphasizes high-sensitivity PCF-SPR biosensors, autonomous systems, and intelligent sensing.
